## 🎯 Target Tasks & Success Definitions

### Task 1: Troubleshooting Coding Errors (Collaborate with AI)
* **What Success Looks Like:** I use Claude to explain why the error occurred, understand the fix, and resolve the bug in under 5 minutes without breaking other parts of my notebook. 

### Task 2: Drafting Professional IT Work Templates (Delegate with Review)
* **What Success Looks Like:** Creating clear, step-by-step resolution emails for common network port issues so the customer resolves their issue on the first try without needing to email back for clarification.

### Task 3: Structuring Weekly Internship Write-ups (Collaborate with AI)
* **What Success Looks Like:** Turning my raw weekly notes into a clean, professional, and readable paragraph for the InternHQ review board that gets approved on the first pass with zero requests for revision.
